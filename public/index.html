Programme Overview

Promoting Reading Culture & Creative Storytelling

The Kid Authors Challenge is a flagship initiative by Davsol Education and Givers Foundation, in conjunction with Rich More Multistream, designed to inspire young minds across Lagos State. We believe every child has a story worth telling — and we're building the platform to help them tell it.

This programme encourages originality, imagination, and excellence in communication, helping students develop the confidence to express themselves through the written word.

Rules & Requirements

Writing Guidelines

Please read carefully before submitting your story.

1

Prose Form Only

All entries must be in prose — no poetry or play scripts.

2

Original Work

Stories must be entirely your own creation. Plagiarism leads to disqualification.

3

Minimum Length

At least 6 pages of typed content.

4

Subject Areas

Stories may be based on Art, Commercial, Science, or combined subjects.
